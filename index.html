What is this website?

This website was born out of the idea to create a database of public parks and hiking areas that are unique to Austin so that we all have something to look back at fondly when we graduate from UT Austin. We expanded on this idea by adding seperate sections for events, restaurants and historical sites since Austin is known for its amazing food, music venues, and for being the capitol of Texas.
